# Connect More - App Store Submission Pages

This document lists all the pages created for Apple App Store submission requirements.

## Marketing & Support Pages

### 1. Landing Page
**Route:** `/connect-more-landing`

A professional marketing page showcasing Connect More's features:
- Hero section with clear value proposition
- Feature highlights with icons
- Statistics section
- How it works guide
- Call-to-action buttons
- Footer with contact information

### 2. Support Page
**Route:** `/connect-more-support`

Comprehensive support center including:

- Contact form for user inquiries
- Professional support contact information

### 3. Privacy Policy
**Route:** `/privacy-policy`

Complete privacy policy covering:
- Information collection practices
- How user data is used and protected

**Route:** `/terms-of-service`

Comprehensive terms including:

## App Store Submission Requirements

### Apple Requirements Met:
✅ App description and marketing page
✅ Support contact information (support@connectmore.app)
✅ Comprehensive FAQ section
✅ Privacy Policy page
✅ Terms of Service page
✅ Professional design and user experience
✅ Clear navigation and accessibility

### Key Features for Review:
1. All pages are fully accessible without login
2. Professional, clean design suitable for business use
3. Clear contact methods for user support
4. Legal compliance documentation
5. Transparent information about the service
